ATVs vs UTVs

What kind of vehicle do you want to drive and what is your budget for the rental?

ATV Rentals

UTV Ride


UTV rentals are most expensive but also the most comfortable and the easiest to ride of all the off road vehicles. (These are what you will drive if you book one of our experiences).

Renting a UTV vs Guided Tours: Which is Right for You?

A guided tour means that an experienced guide is driving a vehicle in front of you and you follow him.

A rental means you explore the desert on your own.

If you decide to rent a side by side (with a company that does not offer guides) and explore the desert on your own, take the time to look at the trail you will be following and bring a lot of water.
